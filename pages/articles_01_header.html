Collapsible Mega Menu A collapsible mega menu is useful if you have a lot of items in your menu. The advantage of this approach is that it won’t cover up any page content. Instead, it’ll push the page down when the menu opens so that all content remains visible. Not only that, but users can move their mouse anywhere and the menu won’t close on them. This means no more hover tunnels or hard-to-click peripheral items.
