// Faaaaar too loud

Let's not mute some content key components like best-buys tables or direct access to products with a navbar that doesn't directly converts. Layout, colours and typography have a huge influence on what the eye is drawn to, and consequently how information is consumed.

Remind our users where they are at at all times. We do this with breadcrumbs, but it’s also important to make sure your main menu uses active states. Our main menu should highlight the pages you are on at the moment.
